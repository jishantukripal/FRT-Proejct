Frequently Asked Questions

How can I be a part of this Club?

Become an FRT alumnus or else contact to any of the members of the clib.

What is the fee to join the Club?

There is no membership fee to join the club.

What is the cost of participating in course?

All the events are free of cost. You just need to be the part of the Club.

Anyone can be member of this club?

The only eligibility criteria to join the club is to be a regular student of any tech courses from an accrediated university.

How to suggest anything?

If you have anything to suggest, then you can mail us at support@futurereadytalent.in.

Any doubt still unanswered?

For any more clarification, contact to team of the Club.
